About Us

Our mission is to connect female drivers with female passengers, creating a safe and respectful environment where women can feel truly comfortable during every ride.
Whether you’re commuting to work, heading out for a night with friends, or simply running errands, Butler@HerTaxis ensures your journey is smooth, supportive, and secure—all while helping women thrive in the transportation industry.

Ready to ride with confidence? Join our growing community today—book your first trip or sign up as a driver and be part of the change

Our Services

  • On-demand and scheduled bookings
  • Driver support and onboarding
